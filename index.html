Tribute to A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J Abdul Kalam

A.P.J Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India," was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of Indiaand was a renowned scientist, know for hiswork in India's space and missile programs.


Achievements

  • developed India's first satelite launch vechicle (SLV-III)
  • Led india's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
